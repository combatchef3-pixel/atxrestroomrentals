Frequently Asked Questions

How many portable restrooms do I need for my event?

The number depends on guest count, duration, and whether food or drinks are served. A common guideline is to increase units for larger crowds.

Can I rent portable toilets for just one day?

Yes. Many providers allow single-day rentals for events, weddings, and short gatherings.

When should I book portable toilets for my event?

Booking at least a few days in advance is ideal, but larger events should reserve units as early as possible to guarantee availability.

How often are portable toilets serviced during a rental?

Servicing frequency varies by event needs and rental duration, but weekly cleaning and restocking is common for longer rentals.

Do ADA compliant units accommodate wheelchairs?

Yes. ADA compliant porta potties have wider entrances and safety features to support wheelchair users.

Wedgefield, SC Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Wedgefield is an unincorporated community in the High Hills of Santee area in western Sumter County, South Carolina, United States. Wedgefield is also a Census Designated Place (CDP). Wedgefield was so named because its location was likened to a "wedge" into the High Hills of Santee. In the 18th and 19th centuries it was the location of many plantations, notably those of the Singleton family, which produced First Lady of the United States, Angelica Singleton Van Buren. Today the plantations are gone except for their cemeteries and Wedgefield is today noted as the location of a state park, a state forest and other recreational places. When the railroad came through in the 19th century, the commercial center of Wedgefield developed at the intersection of the railroad, Kings Highway (South Carolina Highway 261) and Wedgefield Road (South Carolina Highway 763). It has its own post office with the Zip Code of 29168.
