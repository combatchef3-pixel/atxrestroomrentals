Frequently Asked Questions

How many portable restrooms do I need for my event?

The number depends on guest count, duration, and whether food or drinks are served. A common guideline is to increase units for larger crowds.

Can I rent portable toilets for just one day?

Yes. Many providers allow single-day rentals for events, weddings, and short gatherings.

When should I book portable toilets for my event?

Booking at least a few days in advance is ideal, but larger events should reserve units as early as possible to guarantee availability.

How often are portable toilets serviced during a rental?

Servicing frequency varies by event needs and rental duration, but weekly cleaning and restocking is common for longer rentals.

Do ADA compliant units accommodate wheelchairs?

Yes. ADA compliant porta potties have wider entrances and safety features to support wheelchair users.
